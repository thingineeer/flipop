One tap flips the blocks! Match a row to clear — an addictive puzzle game

FLIPOP is a simple yet deep puzzle game where tapping a block changes the colors of surrounding blocks. Fill a horizontal row with the same color to clear it! Chain combos together for an electrifying rush.

Play with adorable character blocks — Cat (red), Dog (blue), Rabbit (yellow), and Frog (green)!

--- How to Play ---
1. Tap a block to change the colors of surrounding blocks
2. Fill a horizontal row with the same color to clear it
3. Create chain reactions for combos and aim for a high score

--- Key Features ---
• Intuitive tap controls — easy for anyone to pick up
• Chain combo system — one tap can trigger a cascade of clears
• 4 cute animal character blocks
• Compete on the global leaderboard
• Watch rewarded ads for bonus rewards
