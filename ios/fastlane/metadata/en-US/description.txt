Tap to flip colors! Match lines to pop in this addictive puzzle game

FLIPOP is a simple yet deep puzzle game where tapping a block changes the colors of surrounding blocks. Fill a horizontal row with the same color to POP! Chain combos together for an electrifying rush.

Play with adorable animal character blocks — Cat (red), Dog (blue), Rabbit (yellow), and Frog (green)!

--- How to Play ---
1. Tap a block to cycle the colors of adjacent blocks (up, down, left, right)
2. Fill a horizontal row with the same color to clear it
3. Create chain reactions for combos and aim for a high score
